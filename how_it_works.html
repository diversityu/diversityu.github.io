Recommendation

Apple considers itself a leader in the technology industry. Hence, as such it should also push the technology industry towards greater diversity. Therefore, we propose an integrated web-based platform to increase diversity in the Apple and, in the future, the whole technology industry. This platform will be built for high school minorities currently in the 10th, 11th and 12th grade. It will be since grade 10 as it has been shown that early engagement is key towards the development of minorities in the technology industry. The platform will serve as a bridge of communication between students and Apple (an in the future other technology companies). Through the platform, students will be able to apply for job postings, contact recruiters (which specialize in diversity inclusion) and communicate with prominent minority figures in the technology industry. This will in turn motivate and engage with minorities from a young age and attract their talents towards the technology industry.
